# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Indian families, multi-generational. The first user is the family's "finance keeper" (often a son or daughter in their 20s or 30s) who sets the app up; secondary users are parents, spouses, and siblings with widely varying financial literacy, often on mid-range Android phones. Onboarding is frequently done on a phone handed over by the finance keeper ("amma, fill this"). The job: give a personal AI financial advisor enough family context to be genuinely useful, in minutes, without it feeling like paperwork.

## Product Purpose

A personal family financial advisor with a durable memory layer. Onboarding seeds that memory (who's who, goals, rough money picture, revealed risk stance); conversation refines it forever. Success: a family member finishes setup in under 5 minutes, feels the app already "gets" their family, and a non-savvy parent never feels talked down to or interrogated.

## Brand Personality

Warm, familial, reassuring. Like a thoughtful family journal, not a fintech dashboard. Speaks everyday Indian English (EMI, SIP, FD are fine; "liabilities" and "portfolio" are not). Calm confidence, never urgency. Gentle delight, never gimmick.

## Anti-references

- Bank portals and government forms (dense fields, ALL-CAPS labels, dropdown walls)
- Generic fintech: navy + gold, neon-on-black crypto, "AI purple" gradients
- SaaS onboarding cliches: identical card grids, hero metrics, progress gamification badges
- Cartoonish "friendly fintech" (oversized illustrations, confetti everywhere)

## Design Principles

1. **Taps over typing.** Every question earns its keystroke; defaults and sliders do the work.
2. **Rough is welcome.** The interface constantly reassures that estimates are fine and everything is editable later in chat.
3. **Warm dark, pastel light.** One unified warm charcoal base; a Pantone-pastel palette provides identity, wayfinding (one pastel per phase), and personality (one pastel per family member).
4. **The family is the hero.** People, names, and relationships get the visual richness; numbers stay quiet and tabular.
5. **Unseen details compound.** Custom easing, origin-aware motion, pressed states everywhere; nothing stock, nothing default.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Touch targets 44px minimum; thumb-reachable primary actions.
- Legible for older eyes: 15px+ body, strong contrast of ink on dark, never gray-on-gray for essential text.
- prefers-reduced-motion respected for all movement.
- No jargon; no em dashes in any user-facing copy (enforced by `npm run check:copy`).
